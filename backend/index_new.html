🔍 What the Extension Does

Permissions needed:

  • "bookmarks" - Read bookmark titles and URLs (domains only extracted for AI)
  • "activeTab" - Only to show the popup when you click the icon

What we send to AI:

  • ✓ Bookmark titles (e.g., "GitHub Repository")
  • ✓ Domains only (e.g., "github.com" not "/user/repo/issues/123")

What we DON'T access:

  • ❌ No "tabs" permission (can't see what you're browsing)
  • ❌ No "history" permission (can't see where you've been)
  • ❌ No background scripts tracking you
